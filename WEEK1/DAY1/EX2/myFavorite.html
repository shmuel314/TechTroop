About Me

My name is Shmuel Harel, and I'm a Full-Stack Developer. I began my career in a completely different field—carpentry—but my curiosity and love for technology led me to transition into software development. I enjoy creating elegant, user-friendly web applications and constantly seek to improve my coding skills.

After completing a Full-Stack course, I joined a FinTech startup founded by my brother. There, I took a leading role in building Angular front-end features and integrating them with a NestJS backend. Working in a small team gave me a wide range of responsibilities, which helped me grow rapidly as a developer.

I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science at the Open University. Despite being busy with work and military reserve duty, I remain dedicated to my studies. I thrive on learning new things and am particularly interested in clean architecture, reactive programming, and improving accessibility in web applications.
