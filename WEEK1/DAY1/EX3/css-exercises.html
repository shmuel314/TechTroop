Shmuel’s Favorite Dish: Shakshuka

Shakshuka is a traditional Israeli dish made with eggs poached in a spicy tomato sauce. It’s perfect for breakfast, lunch, or dinner!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2–4 eggs
  • Fresh parsley (optional)
